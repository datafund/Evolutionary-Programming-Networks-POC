How It Works

Programs as Digital DNA: Each colored dot represents a program with genetic traits like energy efficiency, cooperation level, and reproduction rate.

Natural Selection: Programs compete for limited resources. Those with better fitness survive and reproduce, passing their traits to offspring.

Mutation & Evolution: New programs inherit parent traits but with random mutations, leading to gradual evolution of the population.

Emergent Behaviors: Watch as cooperative clusters form, aggressive programs dominate temporarily, then get outcompeted by more efficient variants.
